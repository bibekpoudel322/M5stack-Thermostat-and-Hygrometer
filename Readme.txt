1)Turn on the M5stack by pressing power button for 2 seconds.
2)Wait for 5 seconds for initial setup to load up. You can see the current humidity, temperature and time in the screen. 
3)Press Middle button to get into menu system. Use Button A or Button B to navigate through the button. Press Middle button to select your option.
4)Based on the settings you got into you can set your own temperature or humidity or calibrate the sensor.
5)Exit the settings by selecting exit settings option to save the settings.
6)Connect your heating,cooling,humidifying and dehumidifying device in the relay 1, relay 2, relay 3, relay 4 respectively. 
7)Go to https://thingspeak.com/channels/2043181 to monitor the data remotely.
